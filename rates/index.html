Please contact me by phone or send me an email outlining the work you would like done on your document.

I charge an hourly rate or can give a whole project quote.

Hourly rate:

  • Basic proofread: AU$20–25 per hour
  • Copyedit: AU$35 per hour

I can quote for the whole project after assessing the document. The rate will be an estimation of the hours needed to do the work, based on the degree of copyediting and proofreading needed, the amount of fact checking required, the length of the document and the time frame for completion.

Special rates can be discussed for long projects and for proofreading following a copyedit.

I will provide a written brief specifying the work you want done, with a firm hourly or whole project quote and completion date, which we will both sign once agreed on.

Any changes to the brief after it has been agreed on that entail extra work will be charged for at an hourly rate.
