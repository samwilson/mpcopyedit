All written material can be improved by copyediting, including:

  • academic assignments, dissertations, exegeses and theses—using relevant citation and referencing systems and following the Institute of Professional Editors’ Guidelines for editing research theses
  • fiction and non-fiction manuscripts
  • newspaper copy
  • journal and magazine articles
  • newsletters
  • business reports
  • educational material
  • research reports
  • marketing material
  • manuals
  • online text
  • lectures
  • podcasts

I can work on hard copy/printed documents, using professional proofreading marks. Alternatively I can work on electronic copies and web text, using Track Changes or correction reports.
